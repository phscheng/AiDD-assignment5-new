About Me

My journey combines academic excellence with real-world experience at Fortune 500 companies.

With expertise in problem-solving and analysis, I enjoy tackling complex challenges and turning ideas into reality. I believe in the power of continuous learning and staying up-to-date with the latest technologies and industry trends.

My Background

My professional journey began with a strong foundation in supply chain management and business analytics at Indiana University's Kelley School of Business. Through internships at Target Corporation and Merck & Co., I've gained hands-on experience in data analysis, process improvement, and strategic decision-making.

At Target, I led process improvements for import inventory management, increasing purchase order accuracy and reducing out-of-stock rates by approximately 5%. At Merck, I audited 7,000+ procurement records to identify compliance risks and generated cost-saving insights across $15M+ in contracts.
