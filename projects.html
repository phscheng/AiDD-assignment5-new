IT Strategy - Kroger & Blockchain

This project proposed using blockchain to enhance Kroger’s supply chain transparency, security, and efficiency. By integrating Oracle Blockchain-as-a-Service with existing IoT and analytics systems, it aimed to create tamper-proof records, automate compliance, and enable real-time traceability across the network. Interactive features like QR codes would let customers trace product origins and provide feedback, strengthening trust, accountability, and brand loyalty while driving long-term adaptability.

Chamber Music - Brahms String Quartet

As part of my undergraduate minor at the Jacobs School of Music, I performed as the cellist in Brahms’ Clarinet Quintet in B Minor, Op. 115. This project deepened my understanding of chamber music collaboration, interpretation, and ensemble balance. Through extensive rehearsals and performance preparation, I focused on achieving cohesive phrasing and tone blending across the strings and clarinet, while exploring the emotional depth and intricate dialogue that define Brahms’ late chamber works.
